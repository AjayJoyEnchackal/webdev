ABOUT

The Robotics Boot Camp is an all-Kerala pioneering event organized by The Robotics Society ASIET chapter and International Society Of Automation ASIET chapter to reveal the horizons of the world of robotics and automation to young minds. This boot camp, which is regarded as Kerala's biggest-ever boot camp, is infused with a plethora of exhilarating activities akin to enlightening technical talk sessions and hands-on demonstrations of modernistic technologies along with illuminating cultural activities. This boot camp endeavors to motivate future robotics and automation engineers to realize their intrinsic interests, to experiment and explore as well as to connect with the community of like minds. It lays out an environment for bright minds to get out of their comfort zone and explore the world beyond.
